## Description

**Every big decision, clearly.**

BunkerMac transforms how you make important choices. Stop second-guessing. Stop agonizing. Start deciding — with clarity, structure, and AI-powered insight.

**Command Center for Your Decisions**
Organize every significant choice in one powerful workspace. BunkerMac's dark, focused interface keeps you in command — not distracted by noise.

**Structured Decision-Making**
- Define criteria that matter to you (with weighted importance)
- Score each option against every criterion
- See ranked outcomes with confidence scores instantly

**AI That Challenges You**
- AI analysis surfaces blind spots and counter-arguments
- "What if" scenario simulator shows how changes ripple through your decision
- Not just answers — better thinking

**Share & Collaborate**
- Invite trusted advisors to score and comment
- Decision Rooms for team voting
- Export decisions as JSON or professional PDF

**For Decisions That Matter**
Low stake or critical — BunkerMac brings the same rigor. Because the small decisions compound, and the big ones define your life.
