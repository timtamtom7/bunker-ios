# App Store Listing - Bunker

## App Name
**Bunker** — AI Decision Coach

## Subtitle
Think clearly. Decide confidently.

## Description
Bunker is an AI-powered decision workspace that helps you make better choices by structuring your thinking, weighing criteria objectively, and simulating outcomes before you commit.

**How it works:**
1. Define what matters — add criteria like Cost, Quality, Risk, or Time
2. Weigh your priorities — rate how important each criterion is
3. Score your options — evaluate each choice against your criteria
4. Get AI-powered recommendations — see the best option with confidence scores

**Key Features:**
- Weighted decision matrix with AI insights
- What-if scenario analysis to test assumptions
- Risk assessment for high-stakes choices
- Decision templates for recurring choices
- Calendar sync for deadlines
- Group decisions by category
- Achievement badges for consistent decision-making
- Export decisions as PDF or JSON
- Dark mode optimized for focused thinking

**Perfect for:**
- Career and job changes
- Investment decisions
- Business strategy
- Major purchases
- Life planning
